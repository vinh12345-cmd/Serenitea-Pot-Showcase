Pre-requirements

Unlock Requirements: After reaching Adventure Rank 28 + and completing the Archon Quest Chapter I: Act III - A New Star Approaches, the World Quest A Teapot to Call Home: Part I will be unlocked. Complete this quest to obtain the Serenitea Pot.

Using the Serenitea Pot

  • Place the Serenitea Pot gadget on a valid surface and interact with it to enter the teapot.
  • If a Sub-Space Waypoint is placed inside the realm, they can be teleported to via the Serenitea Pot Map Tab.
  • The Mansion can be teleported to from the Map as well.
  • While inside the teapot, placing and interacting with the Serenitea Pot gadget or teleporting to a Teleport Waypoint in Teyvat will exit the teapot.

Note: You can only build the Replica ID when you own more than 90% of the furnishing.

Guide

Mobile Device Guide

This section contains guides specifically for mobile users of Serenitea Pot.

How to use Replica ID:

  • Select the Replica ID that you wanted and copy it, then enter the Serenitea Pot. When you are inside the Serenitea Pot, tap on the on the upper right corner of the screen.
  • After that, choose "Replica"
  • After that, choose "Use Replica ID"
  • After that, you can paste the Replica ID by pressing the paste button then "Search"
  • After all that, you can now transfer the replica and start building it

PC Guide

This section contains guides specifically for PC users of Serenitea Pot.

How to use Replica ID:

  • Select the Replica ID that you wanted and copy it, then enter the Serenitea Pot. When you are inside the Serenitea Pot, press F6.
  • After that, click on "Replica"
  • After that, click on "Use Replica ID"
  • After that, paste the Replica ID by pressing the paste button or Ctrl + V then click on "Search"
  • After all that, you can now transfer the replica and start building it
